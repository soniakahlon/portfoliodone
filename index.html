ABOUT ME


Striped Egg: A story of form and function

A little bit about me! I am an orthodontist turned photographer who developed passion for front-end web development while building my photography portfolio website.

Sounds like a lot right? Fortunately, all these streams flow into the same lake of organized creativity.

I am intensely dedicated to functional esthetics and understand the importance of usability in addition to dazzling style.

I use my understanding of analytics in combination with photographic composition techniques to inspire my vision.

I hope that you find my work compelling and refreshing but most of all enjoyable to interact with.
